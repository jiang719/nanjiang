About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student majoring in Computer Science at Purdue University. Currently, I am working with Prof. Lin Tan about leveraging natural language processing techniques on automatic program repair. Specifically, we are trying to build an deep-learning-based encoder-decoder tool to generate patches for given faulty programs. My research interests mainly focus on applying deep learning techniques to help software testing, software repair, and software analysis.
